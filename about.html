INTRODUCING OUR CHEFS!

Great food starts from great people.

Baking with Passion! That is how we run this bakery. Our team of chefs are committed and passionate in what they do, and their love and care are directly passed down to the food we make. We are proud of our chefs - they always strive to serve delicious and wholesome food, bringing the joy of eating to everyone. We want to share special moments with our beloved customers through catering!

We get our ingredients locally. We also minimize unnecessary usage of food to support the environment. We cater community events to celebrate them together with customers.

HOW THEY WORK BEHIND THE SCENE

Our #1 priority is our customers.

Our bakery opens at 6 A.M. sharp. Our chefs start their day by making sure the cooking space is clean and safe. We sanitize the work environment and use fresh ingredients every day to ensure the food is top-quality. All of our products are baked fresh in the morning! But don't worry, our diligent staff will continue baking so there will always be enough food for everyone.

If you are stopping by to grab a few desserts, feel free to say hi to our amazing team of chefs behind the counter! This cheers them up during the day!

Interested in learning more about our baking process? We encourage you to check out our social media to see how we operate behind the scene! Don't forget to give us a like when you are there, thank you so much.
